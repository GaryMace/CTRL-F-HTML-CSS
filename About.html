Welcome to Swimtastic.ie,

Swimtastic.com is solely based around the olympic swimming events. On this site you will find statistics and data in relation to swimming. Our sole goal is to provide you, the public, with useful information about some of the most decorated and talented swimming athletes out there.

My goal as this websites Author is to give the public ease of access to the records and information of pervious swimming stars. As a swimming enthusiast myself I was particularly excited for this project and therefore am glad to share its content with you.
